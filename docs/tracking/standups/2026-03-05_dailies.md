### IO-E Implementation: Complete (3 sub-phases)

**IO-E1: Protocol Definitions** (commit `54071c5`)
- Created `lib/prologos/core/io-protocols.prologos` with 4 session protocols: `FileRead`, `FileWrite`, `FileAppend`, `FileRW`
- 11 tests in `test-io-session-01.rkt`: parsing, duality, structure validation, recursive branches, invalid branch errors

**IO-E2: Session-Based File IO** (commit `fe6ea88`)
- Major architectural discovery: flat propagator networks cannot guarantee IO operation ordering ‚Äî bridge propagators fire during quiescence in unpredictable order, causing close-before-write
- Solution: Direct IO execution during `compile-live-process` compilation walk. IO side effects (file reads/writes/closes) execute inline, not via deferred bridge propagators
- `io-infer-mode`: replaces simple `sess-recv-like?` heuristic with choice-branch analysis, including label-based append detection
- Fresh choice cells: recursive session protocols (e.g., FileWrite `:write` then `:close`) need fresh choice cells per `proc-sel` to avoid contradiction
- Added `read+write` and `append` modes to `io-bridge-open-file`
- 10 tests in `test-io-session-02.rkt`

**IO-E3: Protocol Composition Tests** (commit `a2b5c7c`)
